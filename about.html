About Electroternity

What Is Electroternity?

Electroternity is a website that is meant to help protect the environment. Many used electronics are recycled, but very few are reused. By reusing electronics, not only would the environment be less harmed, but also people without the opportunity to obtain devices could receive one.

How This Website's Idea Arose

Have you ever met someone who wants to do almost anything to help the Earth and environment thrive? Well, that's me. And out of my desire to protect the environment, this website was born. Before I get into how this website was created, I want to introduce myself to everyone. I am Jerrick Ban, an average 14 year old, if I say so myself. I love many things, from drawing, videogames, and tennis, to things like computer programming, math, and nature. Enough about myself, now let me talk about how this website was created. I love the environment so much, yet I felt like I was not doing enough to help it. I saw the pollution everywhere and I was(I still am) afraid what the destiny of the world was. Therefore, using my computer programming skills, which was not much, I developed this website using html/css, bootstrap, and php. I knew that I cannot just sit there and let the pollution and other environmental hazards take control over the world. I felt like it was my duty to protect the earth. However, in reality, it is impossible for one person to change our world alone. All of humanity must cooperate with each other. So please, I challenge you all, including myself of course, that no matter your race, culture, location, or any other factor, try to do environmental friendly activites, for my sake, for your sake, and for the sake of our world. After all, the only way to change the world for the better, is if you change yourself for the better. I hope that this website will help improve the environment at least just a little. If you read through all of that, I really appreciate it! Thanks!

How Electroternity Works

The website works by enabling a member to post an item up on the web for either giveaway or trade. Then, any other people who are interested in the item can contact the poster through his or her email or phone number -- this should be located in the details section -- and make an agreement for what to do with him or her. Honesty is encouraged, for the feelings of the people are respected in our community. For any questions or complaints, please email me with the email at the bottom of the page. And for more detail, visit the other informational pages on the site. Hope you enjoy!
